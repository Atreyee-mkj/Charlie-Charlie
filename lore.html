The Legend of Charlie

They say Charlie was once a boy who spoke to spirits through the simplest of things — a pencil, a whisper, a dare. Every time someone asks, “Charlie, Charlie, are you there?”, the veil thins. The more you ask, the more real he becomes...

They say Charlie was once a boy trapped between worlds — neither spirit nor shadow. His whispers linger in the air where games blur with belief. Children once called to him for answers... but few remember what they asked.

Those who mocked the ritual claimed the pencils moved on their own. Those who didn’t — never spoke again. Charlie listens. Always.
